From Gene to Vault

Our production process is managed under sanitary, climate-controlled conditions, while data and traceability are handled from our central office hub — ensuring full pharmaceutical alignment.

1. Genetic Selection & Cloning

Every batch begins with precision. We select mother plants based on genetic stability, chemotype consistency, and therapeutic potential. Cloning is carried out within Greenhouse 1, under clean conditions that ensure traceability and genetic fidelity — a cornerstone of pharmaceutical-grade cultivation.

2. Nursery & Vegetative Growth

Still within Greenhouse 1, clones enter our nursery and vegetative development phase. Each environmental variable — light spectrum, humidity, nutrient profile — is fine-tuned to optimize growth, immunity, and uniformity. This is where we nurture potential with intention.

3. Flowering (Tunnels 1 & 2)

In the flowering tunnels, plants express their full biochemical potential. Cannabinoid and terpene profiles are shaped here, under tightly controlled and continuously logged conditions. Every lot is monitored in real time, ensuring repeatability and compliance with international quality standards.

4. Slow Curing

After harvesting at peak maturity, flowers undergo a controlled curing process in climate-stabilized rooms. This stage is essential — it allows volatiles to stabilize and bioavailability to mature. More than a technical step, curing honors the rhythm of the plant.

5. Trimming & Finishing

Once cured, flowers are hand-finished inside our central building. Trimming is conducted by trained professionals with pharmaceutical standards in mind — ensuring optimal appearance, dosage uniformity, and absence of contaminants. Every detail counts.

6. Secure Storage & Lot Management

Finished products are vacuum-sealed, cataloged, and stored in secure chambers with environmental control and access monitoring. Each lot is traceable by code, with full documentation, batch analytics, and certificates ready for distribution or inspection. A restricted vault secures premium and high-sensitivity genetics.

Why It matters

Compliance is built into every step. Green Rocket's production model is not just optimized — it's regulated. We follow Good Agricultural and Collection Practices (GACP), align with EU-GMP expectations, and operate under South Africa's Plant Improvement Act and Section 21/22 licensing.Every process is tracked, documented, and validated — from stem cell to storage.
