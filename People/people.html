Leadership that delivers trust

Green Rocket is led from Germany, ensuring regulatory excellence and operational precision. Our management structure brings together global oversight with on-the-ground execution — aligning with European standards while remaining deeply connected to the South African landscape. Every decision is guided by compliance, consistency, and long-term value creation. From strategic planning to daily operations, leadership is embedded in every process — not just in names, but in systems.

Science driven

Behind every Green Rocket strain, there's a team of scientists, agronomists, and geneticists ensuring consistency, safety, and innovation.

Our experts are responsible for developing and refining the K25 strain (formerly Blood Bath), running tissue culture protocols, and ensuring meristem remediation — unlocking genetic stability and pathogen-free propagation.

Whether it's scaling our nursery, optimizing light cycles, or safeguarding genetic IP, their work is the foundation of our pharmaceutical-grade cannabis supply chain.

Governance & local integration

In South Africa, we maintain full regulatory compliance under the Plant Improvement Act and Sections 21 & 22 licensing frameworks. We engage with academic institutions, legal consultants, and local regulators to ensure transparent and sustainable operations. Our governance model reflects international standards — while respecting local ecosystems and social impact.
